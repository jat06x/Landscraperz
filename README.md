🚀 Landscraperz

🌍 Overview

Landscraperz is a real-time local services marketplace that connects customers with skilled professionals for landscaping, roofing, renovations, and home services.

The platform enables users to post jobs and get matched with available contractors quickly, creating a streamlined flow from request to completion.

⸻

🧠 Vision

To simplify how real-world work gets done by building a fast, trusted, and structured connection between people who need work done and people who can do the work.

Landscraperz aims to become the core system for coordinating local services in everyday life.

⸻

⚡ Problem

Finding reliable local service providers today is often slow, fragmented, and inconsistent.

Customers face:

* difficulty finding trusted professionals
* slow response times
* unclear pricing and communication gaps

Contractors face:

* inconsistent job flow
* limited visibility
* inefficient customer acquisition

⸻

💡 Solution

Landscraperz introduces a structured marketplace that improves how local services are discovered and delivered.

Key ideas:

* Instant job posting system
* Smart contractor matching based on availability and skill
* Direct communication between users
* Simplified service workflow from request to completion

⸻

🧩 Core Concept

A unified system where local service demand and supply meet in real time.

Users don’t search endlessly — they submit a request and receive active responses from available professionals.
